Appellate Authority

  • Any person aggrieved by a decision of the certification agency under Section 9 or 10 may appeal to the Appellate Authority by paying the prescribed fees within 30 days from the date on which the decision is communicated..

  • The Appellate Authority in respect of the Puducherry Seed Certification Agency is constituted as below vide G.O. Ms. No 17/Ag dated 12th January 2000:.

  • 1. Chief Secretary to Government, Puducherry Chairman.

  • 2. Dean, PAJANCOA & RI, Karaikal Member.

  • 3. An expert nominated by the cheif Secretary Member
